Claude Shot

Press ⌘⇧S to send a screenshot to Claude instantly, or click the toolbar icon to crop first.

When on, Send activates your terminal and pastes the screenshot for you, no manual ⌘V. Requires running host/scripts/install.sh and granting Accessibility.

The app must be running with a Claude (Code) session focused. In VS Code / Cursor, click into the terminal pane first. ⌘V into the editor pane will paste into the editor.
