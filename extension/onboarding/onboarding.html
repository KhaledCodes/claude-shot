Welcome to Claude Shot

Screenshot any tab and send it straight into Claude.

Your shortcut ⌘+⇧+S (Ctrl+Shift+S on Windows/Linux)
  1. Press ⌘⇧S to screenshot the visible tab and open a preview. That's the fastest way. You can also click the toolbar icon. Change the shortcut
  2. Crop a region if you want, then hit Send to Claude. The image lands on your clipboard, ready to paste into Claude (claude.ai, Claude Desktop, or Claude Code).
  3. Optional, in settings: paste directly into a claude.ai tab, or auto-paste into your terminal. Pin the icon too (puzzle-piece in the toolbar, then the pin) so it's always one keystroke away.
