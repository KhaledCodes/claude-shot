Welcome to Claude Shot

Screenshot any tab and send it straight into Claude.

Your shortcut ⌘+⇧+S (Ctrl+Shift+S on Windows/Linux)
  1. Press ⌘⇧S and it's sent to Claude instantly. No preview, no extra clicks. That's the whole point. By default the screenshot is copied to your clipboard, ready to paste. Turn on the opt-ins below to drop it straight into claude.ai or your terminal instead. Change the shortcut
  2. Just want part of the page? Click the icon, then Crop. Drag a region and it sends the instant you're done. Cropping is instant-send too, no extra step. Or click the icon and hit Send to grab the whole tab.
  3. Optional, in settings: paste directly into a claude.ai tab, or auto-paste into your terminal (macOS). Pin the icon too (puzzle-piece in the toolbar, then the pin) so it's always one keystroke away.
