Welcome to Claude Shot

Screenshot any tab and send it straight into Claude.

Your shortcut ⌘+⇧+S (Ctrl+Shift+S on Windows/Linux)
  1. Press ⌘⇧S and it's sent to Claude instantly. No preview, no extra clicks. That's the whole point. Out of the box the screenshot is copied to your clipboard, ready to paste. Change the shortcut
  2. Just want part of the page? Click the icon, then Crop. Drag a region and it sends the instant you're done. Or click the icon and hit Send to grab the whole tab.

One-time setup: screenshots straight into your terminal

Set this up once and ⌘⇧S drops your screenshot right into your Claude Code session, with no ⌘V at all. Works with Terminal, iTerm, VS Code, Cursor, and more. (macOS only.)

  1. Copy the line below, paste it into your Terminal app, and press Return. It quietly sets everything up for you, nothing to build or configure:
    bash <(curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/KhaledCodes/claude-shot/main/host/scripts/install.sh)
  2. Flip on Accessibility for the helper. The installer opens the right System Settings panel; just switch it on there. That's what lets it paste into your terminal for you.
  3. Pick your terminal app in Settings. Auto-paste is already on, so once you see the green dot, you're all set.

Prefer claude.ai? Turn on direct paste in Settings. Pin the icon (puzzle-piece in the toolbar, then the pin) so it's always one keystroke away.
