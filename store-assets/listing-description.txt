Claude Shot is a one-keystroke bridge from your browser to Claude, built for macOS.

Press ⌘⇧S to instantly capture the active tab, no preview and no extra clicks. With the one-time macOS helper installed, it pastes straight into your Claude Code session in your terminal (Terminal, iTerm, VS Code, Cursor, and more), no ⌘V. Without the helper, it's copied to your clipboard so you can paste it into Claude yourself (claude.ai, Claude Desktop, or Claude Code). Want just part of the page? Click the toolbar icon and Crop, and it sends the moment you're done.

Built for people who are constantly screenshotting a page to ask Claude about it.

FEATURES
• Send a screenshot to Claude in one keystroke (⌘⇧S): no preview, no extra clicks.
• Crop to a region at native pixel resolution; the crop sends the moment you finish.
• Two shortcuts: send instantly, or open the popup to crop first.
• Opt-in: paste directly into the claude.ai chat composer when you're already on claude.ai.
• Install the one-time macOS helper and screenshots auto-paste right into your Claude Code session, no ⌘V (on by default once the helper is set up).

PRIVACY
• Your screenshots never leave your device. No servers, no analytics.
• No access to all your websites. Claude Shot only reads a tab when you explicitly invoke it.

WHAT IT CAN'T CAPTURE
Chrome blocks tab capture on chrome:// pages, the Web Store, the new-tab page, and the PDF viewer. Claude Shot shows a notification instead of failing silently.
